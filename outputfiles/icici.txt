Similarly, a P/B ratio around 2.0 suggests that the stock is reasonably valued relative to its book value. Asset quality is monitored through metrics like the Non-Performing Assets (NPA) ratio and Provision Coverage Ratio (PCR). A lower NPA ratio below 2% and a PCR of 70% or higher indicate prudent management of loan assets and provisions for potential losses.

Additionally, technological advancements and competition in the banking sector, both domestically and internationally, play a vital role in shaping ICICI Bank's market position and profitability.
